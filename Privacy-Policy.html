Privacy Policy

Application: MarkOne  |  Last updated: May 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MarkOne ("the App") handles information in connection with its operation. The App is a personal content automation tool operated solely by its developer.

1. No Third-Party Users

MarkOne has no end users other than its developer. The App does not offer a public interface, registration system, or any service accessible to third parties. No personal data from third parties is collected, processed, or stored at any time.

2. Data Collected by the App

The App does not collect personal data from any individual. The only information handled by the App is:

  • OAuth tokens: Access and refresh tokens issued by TikTok and YouTube upon the developer's authorization. These tokens are stored locally on the developer's machine and are never transmitted to any external server other than the respective platform's API endpoints.
  • Content metadata: Titles, descriptions, and hashtags for videos created by the developer. This information is stored locally and uploaded to the developer's own social media accounts.

3. How Data Is Used

OAuth tokens are used exclusively to authenticate API requests made on behalf of the developer to upload and manage content on their own TikTok and YouTube accounts. No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data (tokens, metadata, generated content) is stored locally on the developer's machine. No cloud database or external storage service is used to retain any user or API data. Access is restricted to the developer only.

5. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained locally until manually revoked by the developer or until the token expires per the issuing platform's policies. The developer may delete all local data at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

The App communicates with the following external services on behalf of the developer:

  • TikTok API – for video upload and account management (TikTok Privacy Policy)
  • YouTube Data API v3 – for video upload and channel management (Google Privacy Policy)

The App does not share any data with these services beyond what is strictly necessary for the upload functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
